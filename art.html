Here are some of the courses I have taken related to the Visual Art major:

COMM-372

Branding and Communication was a more business focused course that went into what makes up brands and the design behind them in the economy. In this course I developed my own personal website using Wix, which a lot of artistic design was involved.

FAAR-108

Two-Dimensional Design was an introductory course all about art techniques related to the creation of 2-D work. In this course I learned skills in drawing and painting as we had many projects that allowed us to explore our own creativity with the required medium.

FAAR-215

Mixed Media was a very fun class and allowed me to express my creativity in many ways using a wide variety of materials. We had projects such as creating a book, making artwork out of paper, making artwork out of cloth, and doing earthworks.

FAAR-309

Graphic Design I was an exciting course that allowed me to combine some of my computer science skills and art skills into creating visuals on the computer. In this class we used Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign to create various projects such as how-to-guides, event posters, and small booklets.

ARMO-300

Modernity in Visual Art is a course focused on the evolution of art throughout history to today. I learned a lot about various art movements as well as the impact historic events had on art and how art changed through the years.
