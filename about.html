My Work Ethic

I am someone who is definitely a planner. I typically write down in my agenda all the important, time sensitive things I need to get complete every week. I would also consider myself very proactive about my work. If there is ever a situation to where I am working on something, and I do not understand it or need more help I always ask who I can in order to do what I need to correctly.

I am someone who also prefers working as a team. In any setting I am in it is always better to collaborate with others and improve not only myself but others too. I also like to build strong relationships in the workplace, not just with what is being worked on but truly getting to know and become closer with people I work with.
